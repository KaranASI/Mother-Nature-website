Mother Nature

We cultivate Change, Grow, and Connect — innovative solutions for reforestation and environmental restoration.

Established in 2005, we set out on a mission to make a positive impact on the environment. Our pioneering efforts in reforestation technology began with the groundbreaking product, TerraSeed, launched in 2007. Today, our community of committed environmentalists, conservationists, and nature enthusiasts spans across continents, collectively working towards a greener future.

Creating positive change, whether in our environment or through innovative solutions, requires time, effort, and dedication.

At Mother Nature, our passion is crafting solutions for a greener planet. We are driven by the joy of making a positive impact and serving our global community committed to environmental well-being.

At Mother Nature, we are a diverse team of over 350 individuals hailing from 30 countries, with our headquarters in Bangalore and offices in Los Angeles and Tokyo.

Many of us are passionate about environmental conservation and actively engage in practices to support it. Whether we're environmental scientists, conservationists, or simply nature enthusiasts, our team represents a wide spectrum of cultural and professional backgrounds. What unites us is our shared commitment to leveraging our skills and knowledge to contribute to a sustainable future for our planet.

We believe in focused dedication to our reforestation mission. At Mother Nature, we prioritize quality over quantity, empowering our team with the tools they need to innovate.

We embrace diversity, valuing open debate as essential for driving meaningful progress. Our approach encourages collaboration, ensuring that every voice is heard in our pursuit of a greener future.

We are driven by passion not only for our work but also for continuous personal and professional growth. At Mother Nature, we cultivate an environment that nurtures individual development, fostering opportunities for learning and collaboration.

Through our internal training program and dedicated support, employees are encouraged to expand their knowledge and skills, applying them effectively in their roles. Additionally, our staff-led development initiatives and knowledge-sharing sessions provide avenues for exploring new technologies, production techniques, and best practices in the realm of reforestation.

We're dedicated to ensuring our employees feel valued and supported. As we seek exceptional talent from around the world to join us in Bangalore, we're committed to making your transition as seamless as possible. We provide assistance with relocation logistics and paperwork, along with complimentary language lessons in English or other languages as needed.

Moreover, working in Bangalore offers access to comprehensive health insurance for you and your family, along with generous leave policies. Our flexible office hours promote work-life balance, complemented by company outings and a variety of engaging small-group activities.
We’re really proud of the work we’ve done so far. But there’s so much more to come. If you’d like to be a part of it, please join us.
